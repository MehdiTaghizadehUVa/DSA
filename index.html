Professional 8-Week DSA Roadmap (Free)

Balanced mix of university-grade theory, pattern practice, visual intuition, and graded problemsets. Your progress is saved locally in this browser.
⏱️ 90–120 min/day, 5 days/week
Order rationale: each week layers a **few core patterns**, then immediately **consolidates with practice** and **visuals**. “Depth” items are optional stretch goals or advanced proofs.
How to study — Day 1: 1–2 short lectures → Day 2–3: 4–6 problems in the week’s pattern → Day 4: one harder set → Day 5: 60–75 min timed set. Keep a running notebook: idea → invariant/state → complexity → pitfalls.
